Vale Michael 'Mike' Craig

The Camberwell Hockey Club would like to express our collective sadness at the passing of a club legend and Life Member, Michael Craig, aged 92. Mike Craig was a Life Member of our Club, a Life Member of Hockey Victoria and Life Member of Hockey Australia. Mike’s hockey life was one of extraordinary success and giving.
